Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Time Frame

You have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Required Conditions

  • Product must be unused and undamaged
  • Original tags must be attached
  • All original packaging and accessories must be included
  • Proof of purchase (order number/receipt) is required

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned for health and safety reasons:

  • Personal care items (e.g., electric toothbrushes, razors)
  • Software, DVDs, and video games that have been opened
  • Gift cards and downloadable software products
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

Step 1: Initiate Return

Log into your account and visit the "Order History" section

Step 2: Select Items

Select the item(s) you wish to return and provide a reason

Step 3: Print Label

Print the prepaid return shipping label (if applicable)

Step 4: Package Item

Securely package the item(s) with all original contents

Step 5: Ship Item

Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized shipping location

Return Shipping

We provide free return shipping for:

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Incorrect items received
  • Returns due to our error

For returns based on change of mind, return shipping costs will be deducted from your refund.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. If this isn't possible, we will issue store credit or discuss alternative arrangements.

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability.

Exchange Process

  1. Follow the standard return process to send back the original item
  2. Indicate that you would like an exchange and specify your preferred alternative
  3. We'll ship the replacement item as soon as we process your return

If the exchange item costs more than the returned item, you will be charged the difference. If it costs less, we will refund the difference.

Special Exceptions

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a free pickup and expedite a replacement at no additional cost.

International Returns

International returns may be subject to additional shipping costs and import/export duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Final Sale Items

Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged unless defective.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process my return?

Once we receive your return, it typically takes 2-3 business days to process. You will receive an email confirmation once processing is complete.

What if I lost my receipt?

If you made your purchase online, we can look up your order using your email address. For in-store purchases, we may be able to locate the transaction with your credit card information.

Can I return a gift I received?

Yes, gift returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase. You will receive store credit in the form of a gift card for the current selling price of the item.

What if my return is past the 30-day window?

Returns outside the 30-day window may be accepted at our discretion, typically for store credit only. Please contact our customer service team to discuss your situation.
